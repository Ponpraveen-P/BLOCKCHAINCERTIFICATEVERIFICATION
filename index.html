How It Works

Securing credentials in four transparent cryptographic steps.

1

Certificate Issued

Academic details are inputted and approved by authority.

2

Stored Securely

Metadata records are stored in the local registry database.

3

4

About BlockCert

BlockCert is a next-generation academic credentialing registry designed to eliminate credential fraud. By leveraging cryptographic hashing and decentralized verification workflows, it allows colleges, universities, and technical academies to publish verified accomplishments that are instantly audit-ready.

Our platform generates cryptographically signed PDF certificates containing embedded SHA-256 integrity proofs. Third parties can cross-reference the certificates in a fraction of a second, preventing the distribution of counterfeit diplomas and certifications.
